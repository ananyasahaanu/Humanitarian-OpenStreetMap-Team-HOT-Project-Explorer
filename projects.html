Projects Completed

Over the years, we have completed a variety of projects that aim to make the world a better place. These projects range from humanitarian mapping efforts to advanced technological research initiatives.

Our Key Projects

  • Project Horizon: Mapping disaster-affected areas to provide accurate relief data.
  • EcoMap: Identifying and protecting endangered ecosystems through open data.
  • Urban Growth Monitor: Tracking rapid urbanization and its environmental impacts.
  • Safe Streets: Enhancing pedestrian and cyclist safety through better infrastructure data.
  • HealthReach: Mapping remote health facilities to improve healthcare accessibility.

These projects are just the beginning. Our team constantly works to innovate, adapt, and create meaningful impact through data-driven exploration.
