Milestones Reached

Throughout our journey, we have achieved several incredible milestones that demonstrate our growth, determination, and commitment to making a difference.

Our major milestones include:

- Reaching over 10,000 active explorers contributing to our projects globally.

- Successfully completing 100 humanitarian mapping projects in disaster-affected regions.

- Building a network of 500 global partners working together for sustainable change.

- Launching an open-data initiative that has been adopted by government agencies and NGOs worldwide.

These milestones are not just numbers; they represent the lives impacted, the communities empowered, and the future we are building together.
