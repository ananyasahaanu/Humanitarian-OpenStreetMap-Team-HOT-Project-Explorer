Our Journey So Far

From humble beginnings to mapping thousands of communities worldwide, our volunteers have accomplished incredible milestones. Every building mapped, every road traced, every missing facility located counts towards improving humanitarian response and global resilience.

We celebrate each achievement, whether it's completing a mapping project, reaching a new volunteer goal, or implementing innovative tools to help communities in need.

Key Milestones

1,250+ Explorers Joined

Over 1,250 volunteers from around the globe have contributed their time and skills to humanitarian mapping efforts, from professional cartographers to enthusiastic beginners.

48 Completed Projects

We have successfully completed 48 major mapping projects, providing valuable data to support disaster response, public health planning, and sustainable development initiatives.

96 Celebrated Achievements

Every milestone, whether a small mapping task or a large-scale campaign, is celebrated. Recognition inspires our volunteers to continue making a global impact.

Global Impact Stories

Mapping efforts have helped communities prepare for floods, locate medical facilities, and coordinate aid during disasters. Volunteers have become the eyes on the ground, ensuring no region is left uncharted.

Volunteer Recognition

We honor our volunteers through special programs, certificates, and global shout-outs, motivating them to continue contributing to humanitarian mapping projects.

How You Can Contribute

Every contribution counts! Whether tracing buildings on OpenStreetMap, helping verify field data, or organizing community mapping events, you can make a real difference. Join us and be part of the global volunteer mapping community!
